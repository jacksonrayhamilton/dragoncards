Dragon Cards!

The Dragons have Woken!

Welcome to the open beta of Dragon Cards, a free multiplayer online card game!

How to start a game:

  • Enter your name.
  • Request a duel with someone in the lobby.
  • If they accept, the duel will begin.

How to play:

  • Collect 2 sets of Dragon Cards, summon those dragons, and defeat your opponent's.
  • First, you and your opponent take turns. On your turn, you may:
    • Draw: Draw from the deck and discard.
    • Pilfer: Take the top card from either discard pile and discard.
    • Summon: If you have 2 sets of Dragon Cards (a set is 3 cards of the same element and level) you may summon those dragons. Your opponent then draws until he has a set, and his dragons are summoned too.
  • After summoning, the battle begins. Submit your attacks and wait for your opponent's; the results of the last battle will be shown; like a Pokemon battle. You may:
    • Attack: Attack the opponent dragon in either the 1st or 2nd spot.
    • Switch: Switch spots with your other dragon.
    • Counter: If you are attacked, the attack is negated, and you retaliate against the attacker.
  • Continue submitting attacks until all of your opponent's dragons are dead.

Strategy:

  • There are 5 elements: 木 (Wood), 火 (Fire), 地 (Earth), 金 (Metal), and 水 (Water).
  • Wood beats Earth, Fire beats Metal, Earth beats Water, Metal beats Wood, and Water beats Fire.
  • Levels range from 1 to 5. 1 is the weakest, 5 is the strongest. Strength increases logarithmically.
  • Pay attention to the elements your opponent is collecting so you can counter them.
  • Try to predict your opponent's moves so you can switch and counter accordingly.
  • Have fun.
